##### hawk

US: [hɔk]
UK: [hɔːk]

###### 词义

`a large bird that hunts and eats small birds and animals`

`to try to sell goods, usually by going from place to place and trying to persuade people to buy them`

###### 同义词

vt. 兜售，沿街叫卖；捕捉；咳出
