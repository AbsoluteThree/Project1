##### ignore

US: [ɪɡ'nɔr]
UK: [ɪg'nɔː]

###### 词义

vt. 不顾，不理，忽视
`to deliberately pay no attention to something that you have been told or that you know about`

###### 同义词

vt. 驳回诉讼；忽视；不理睬
`neglect` `pass sb by`


###### 短语

- `ignore all` 全部忽略

###### 例句

`You can’t ignore the fact that many criminals never go to prison.`
你不能无视很多罪犯从未获刑的事实。

`problems which we can’t afford to ignore`
我们不能置之不理的问题
