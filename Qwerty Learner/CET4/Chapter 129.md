##### penalty

US: ['pɛnəlti]
UK: ['penəlti]

###### 词义

n. 处罚，惩罚；罚金
`a punishment for breaking a law, rule, or legal agreement`

###### 同义词

n. [经]罚款，罚金；[法]处罚

###### 短语

- `death penalty` 死刑
- `penalty function` 罚函数；[数]补偿函数
- `penalty for` 罚金；对…的处罚
- `administrative penalty` 行政处罚
- `penalty area` 罚球区，禁区
- `penalty kick` 罚点球，罚球
